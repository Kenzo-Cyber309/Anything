Hardware and Software

In the Hardware and Software department, Android comes on top. Compared to Apple at least. the Iphone 17 is, as of today the latest Apple Tech, and it has 8GB of RAM, and dual 48MP rear cameras. Storage options include 256GB and 512GB

Meanwhile the Google Pixel 10 Pro (Yes I know there are other Androids) have 16GB of RAM, and up to 1TB of storage. Its camera system includes a 50MP wide, 48MP ultrawide, and 48MP telephoto lens on the rear, and a 42MP front camera. Way better.

Or the Samsung? Their latest phone a 200MP main lens, a 50MP ultrawide lens, a 50MP telephoto lens (5x optical zoom), and a 10MP telephoto lens (3x optical zoom), with a 12MP front camera. Available RAM is 12GB (or 16GB), with storage options of 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB, also incredibly good.

Or the Oneplus 13? It has 12GB/16GB RAM and 256GB/512GB storage configurations. The camera system includes a 50MP wide camera with OIS. And that's the lowest of the three.

There's no saving how much better Android Companies are with their software and hardware game.

Innovation

Android also has tons of innovation, way way WAY more than Apple. 100x Zoom, Foldable Phones, The Magic Eraser, Gesture Typing, etc. Compare those innovations with Apple's? Yeah, not a chance.
